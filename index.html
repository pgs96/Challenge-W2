ABOUT ME

My name is Paulina Garza Sandoval. I was born in Monterrey, Mexico on July 31st 1996. I have lived my whole life in Mexico but I love to travel. My favorite traveling destinations are usually beaches, big cities or even amusement parks. My hobbies are watching movies, playing video games, spinning, and analog photography. I also love animals... I have 13 pets, including 10 dogs and 3 cats! I have a degree on graphic design, graduated in 2021. I have worked in many categories from graphic design, such as branding, editorial, event design and UI/UX.
